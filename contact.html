Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with us

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Typically: Websites take 2-4 weeks, Mobile Apps 6-12 weeks, and E-commerce stores 4-8 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the discovery phase.

What's your pricing structure?

We offer transparent, fixed-price quotes based on project requirements. Websites start at $1,500, Mobile Apps at $5,000, and E-commerce solutions at $3,000. All quotes include a detailed breakdown of costs.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes! We offer maintenance packages starting at $150/month. This includes hosting, security updates, bug fixes, and minor updates. We also offer hourly support for ad-hoc needs.

What technologies do you work with?

We work with modern technologies including React, Vue.js, Node.js, Python, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, React Native, Flutter, WordPress, and more. We choose the best stack for each project.

Do you provide design services?

Absolutely! Our UI/UX design services are included in all development projects. We create custom designs that are both beautiful and functional, following user-centered design principles.

What's your development process?

We follow a 4-step process: 1) Discovery & Planning, 2) Design & Prototyping, 3) Development & Testing, 4) Launch & Support. Each phase includes client collaboration and approval.
